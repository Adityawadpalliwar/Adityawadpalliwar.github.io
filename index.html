Who I Am

I'm Aditya Wadpalliwar, a 3rd-year B.Tech ECE student at NIT Trichy, passionate about bridging the gap between hardware and intelligent control systems.

My work spans the full stack of embedded engineering — from schematic design and 4-layer PCB layout in KiCad, to writing tight real-time firmware in C/C++ for STM32 microcontrollers.
